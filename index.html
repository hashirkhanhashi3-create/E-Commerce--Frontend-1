Our Story

Built on the Belief That Quality Endures

LUXE was founded in 2020 with a single conviction: that the most meaningful things you own should be made to last a lifetime — not a season.

We work directly with artisans across Italy, Japan, and the UK. Every piece in our store passes through hands that have spent decades mastering a single craft. No middlemen. No fast fashion. Just the finest materials, shaped with intention.

Our curation process is ruthless. For every item we carry, we've turned down dozens. We look for provenance, durability, and that rare quality that makes an object feel inevitable — like it couldn't have been made any other way.
